Dissertation Research

My dissertation examines how health and mortality risks are associated with alcohol consumption. Structured across three analytical chapters, my dissertation focuses on:

  1. cohort mortality variation and the minimum legal drinking age act (MLDA) of 1984;
  2. recent changes among women’s labor market participation and alcohol consumption related mortality risks; and
  3. physical and mental health outcomes among young adults attributable to drinking and risky behavior.
